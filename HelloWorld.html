Gina Chin

Born and raised in South Korea, Gina is the eldest of three sisters. She lived and studied in Busan, South Korea while her parents operated a restaurant in Seoul, South Korea in order to support the family. Later, Gina went to Taipei, Taiwan to study at the National Taiwan University. However, after the first year, Gina received her geeen card and decided to move to the United States where new opportunities were available.

Family Life

As a wife and a mom, Gina stays active in being part of the family.

Married for 22 years, Gina and Lewis met in Atlanta, GA. Although both originated in Korea (but are of Chinese ancestry), the two met and married in the United States in which one year later, they had their first child, Anthony.

Mother of two, Gina loves spending time with her children, whether it is simply going to the movies or flying all the way to Asia togther. Anthony is a rising Junior at the Georgia Institute of Technology, majoring in Computer Science; Tiffany is a rising Junior at the Gwinnett School of Mathematics, Science, and Technology. Both children have bright futures ahead of them, and Gina is excited to see what is in store for them.

Business Life

Since 2008, Gina has been the owner and operator of Green Tea Restaurant. Before Green Tea, Gina was also the owner of various Chinese cuisines: Golden Palace, Golden Palace Express, Green Tea@Flowery Branch, and Green Tea Diner.
